Welcom to Kit's Page

Aside from being my name, 'kit' is also a word for a young fox

There is also a 'kit fox' that lives in North America

In all cases, kit's are cute!

Some characteristics of the kit fox:

  • They live in North America
  • They are the smallest North American canidae
  • It is a nocturnal animal, but sometimes ventures out during the day
  • They usually establish monogamous matings paris, though pairs sometimes change year to year

What fascinating creatures!

Here is a list of things Kit and kits have in common:

  1. We are both nocturnal, though we may venture out during the day if hungry
  2. We can both be found in North America
  3. We are both generally monogamous, though I don't expect to change my mate next year!
